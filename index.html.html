DIAMOND JUNIOR SCHOOL


Founded in 2009, Diamond Junior School (DJS) is a values-based, globally recognized institution with a focus on equipping and inspiring each child to reach their full,
God-given potential, preparing them for both personal and professional success in the future.
Diamond Junior School is a private school in Nairobi, Kenya, and a pioneer of exceptional modern education in Kindergarten, Primary and Junior Secondary. Students aged from two years and above receive unparalleled independent day education across our two beautiful campuses situated in South B in Nairobi and Crystal Rivers Campus in Athi River.
At Diamond Junior School, our learners are true giants in academics and extracurricular pursuits, consistently demonstrating excellence that shines on both national and international stages. With a commitment to academic rigor, our students achieve outstanding results, setting a high standard for scholarship and intellectual curiosity.

Beyond the classroom, they excel in a range of extracurricular activities—from sports and performing arts to Lego League and science competitions—where they proudly represent our school and country. Whether in academic arenas or extracurricular fields, our students embody the spirit of dedication, talent, and resilience, making them leaders and role models wherever they go.
