Diamond Junior School-South B Campus

Welcome to South B Campus

Our campus provides a serene environment for learning in the midst of the hustle and bustle of the city, complemented by passionate educators who are committed to fostering excellence in the holistic development of every child. We warmly welcome children as young as 2 years old to join our playgroup class, marking the beginning of their educational journey in the foundation stage. From this point, they progressively transition into our Primary School level and eventually our Junior School, experiencing growth and development at every step along the way.
