Welcome to Crystal Rivers


Our campus provides a tranquil environment for learning, complemented by dedicated educators whoare committed to fostering excellence in the holistic development of every child. We warmly welcome children as young as 2 years old to join our playgroup class, marking the beginning of their educational journey in the foundation stage. From this point, they progressively transition into our Primary School level and eventually our Junior School, experiencing growth and development at every step along the way.
